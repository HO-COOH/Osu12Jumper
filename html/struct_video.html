std::string Video::fileName

Location of the background image relative to the originalBeatmap directory.

Double quotes are usually included surrounding the filename, but they are not required.

◆ xOffset

int Video::xOffset

Offset in osu! pixels from the center of the screen.

For example, an offset of 50,100 would have the background shown 50 osu! pixels to the right and 100 osu! pixels down from the center of the screen.

Note
If the offset is 0,0, writing it is optional.
